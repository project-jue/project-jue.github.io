Project-Jue

The AI First Language

Project Jue is an R&D initiative to build a new computational paradigm inspired by the intersection of language, cognition, and formal systems. We are developing a programming language, compiler, and runtime environment designed to be more intuitive and expressive, fundamentally aligning software creation with human thought processes. Our work aims to unlock new possibilities in artificial intelligence and complex systems modeling by bridging the gap between human intent and machine execution.

About the Project

Project Jue originates from a deep-seated inspiration in the cyclical nature of knowledge and innovation, embodied by the concept of 'Zhou' (周). Our vision is to create a computational ecosystem that bridges the gap between human language, cognitive processes, and formal systems. We aim to explore new paradigms in programming that are more intuitive, expressive, and aligned with how we think and communicate, fostering a more natural and profound interaction between humans and machines.
